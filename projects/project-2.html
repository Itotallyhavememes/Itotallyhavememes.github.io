Office Space: Donut King

Made with Unity
Created 7/2024

Office Space is a first-person shooter game set in an office building, where players assume the role of bored office workers engaging in an objective-based battle using common office supplies and toys as imaginary weapons to become the Donut King before their coffee break ends.

Office Space was made as part of our Project and Portfolio II & III classes and marked our first experience working with C# and a game engine. I was responsible for animation implementation and player mechanics. Additionally, I took on various unassigned tasks to support the team, such as programming a vending machine that dispenses random items.
